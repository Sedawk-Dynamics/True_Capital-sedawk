Save your logo image in this folder as exactly:  logo.png

Tips for the best result on the dark header:
- Use a TRANSPARENT background (not white) so it doesn't show as a white box.
- Crop tightly to the logo (remove the big white padding around it).
- A horizontal or mark-only version works best in the navbar.

Once public/logo.png exists, refresh the site — it is picked up automatically.
(Until then, the built-in SVG logo is shown as a fallback.)
